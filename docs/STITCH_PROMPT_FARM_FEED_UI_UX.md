# Stitch (Google) Prompt — Farm Feed UI/UX Design

Copy the prompt below into [Stitch by Google](https://stitch.withgoogle.com/) to generate the landing page and UI design.

---

## Prompt (copy everything below this line)

```
Design a premium, desktop-first landing page and UI for **Farm Feed**: a South African grain and feed marketplace that positions itself as the "Digital Handshake" — "The Pulse of SA Grain. You Trade, We Connect."

**Product in one sentence:** Farm Feed connects farmers, buyers, and transporters in one trusted marketplace. We provide FICA-verified listings, automated contracts, and invoices; users keep the profit and handle payments offline. We are the engine that creates the deal, not a middleman.

**Design system (strict):**
- Primary: Forest Green (#3D693D) — 80% of color use. Use for headers, primary buttons, trust elements, cards.
- Accent: Harvest Red (#DB4A39) — 20%. Use sparingly for CTAs, urgency, or key highlights.
- Neutrals only otherwise (whites, light grays, dark text). No other brand colors.
- Typography: Strong hierarchy, calm spacing, premium SaaS feel. Trust and clarity first. Fewer elements, stronger impact.
- Tone: Professional, confident, agricultural but modern. No generic stock-photo vibe.

**Landing page structure (in order):**

1. **Hero (above the fold — one viewport, no scroll needed)**
   - Full-bleed background: South African grain/farm imagery (harvest, silos, wheat/maize, golden hour). Evocative, not generic.
   - Single compact **action card** in the lower third (frosted dark green overlay or card): 
     - Headline: "SA Grain. You Trade, We Connect."
     - Tagline: "Verified, contracted, paid. You keep the profit."
     - One unmissable primary CTA button: "See live listings"
     - Three secondary pill links: "List grain" | "Browse stock" | "See loads"
     - Trust line: e.g. "500+ traders · R50M+ traded · 4.8/5 · 97% match"
   - Optional: subtle live "pulse" or indicator that the marketplace is active. No clutter; everything fits in one screen.

2. **Three-pillar user flow**
   Three clear portals (cards or sections), not one generic CTA:
   - **I am a Farmer** — "List your grain. Get direct offers." (link: e.g. List grain / register as seller)
   - **I am a Buyer** — "Browse verified stock. Make an offer." (link: Browse stock / listings)
   - **I am a Transporter** — "Quote on loads. Fill your trucks." (link: See loads / transport)
   Use Forest Green and clear hierarchy so each persona sees their path immediately.

3. **Pure Transparency (4-row grid)**
   Headline: e.g. "The Farm Feed way: no surprises."
   Four rows (or cards), one per item:
   - **Verification** — "Every user is FICA-verified before they see a single price. No tire-kickers."
   - **The Deal** — "Smart-Offer System handles bidding. Once accepted, we auto-generate the contract."
   - **Documentation** — "Instant invoices and Sales Agreements, signed digitally on-platform."
   - **The Handover** — "After the contract is signed, you get direct contact details. Logistics and payments happen privately between you."

4. **Problem section**
   Headline: "Stop Chasing Paper. Start Closing Deals."
   Body: "Agriculture in SA moves on trust, but it stalls on admin. Farm Feed digitizes the handshake. We solve finding the right partner and fixing the paperwork. Once the deal is locked, we step aside — you manage logistics and payments your way."
   One CTA: e.g. "Start trading" or "Get started".

5. **New World (four outcomes)**
   Four benefits in a 2x2 or 4-column layout:
   - **Reduced Overhead** — No brokers, no massive commissions.
   - **Speed** — Listing to signed contract in minutes, not days.
   - **Security** — Only vetted, FICA-verified users.
   - **Control** — Payments offline; you keep your banking and accounting.

6. **How it Works (4-step visual)**
   Headline: e.g. "How it works."
   Four steps with clear labels and short copy:
   - **LIST** — Farmer posts FICA-verified grain.
   - **BID** — Buyer makes an offer; Transporter submits a quote.
   - **LOCK** — Farm Feed generates the contract and invoice.
   - **GO** — Parties connect offline. Delivery and payment. Deal done.
   Use a simple horizontal flow or numbered cards. Forest Green accents.

7. **Final CTA**
   Headline: "Ready to connect? You trade, we handle the paperwork."
   Subtext: e.g. "Join 500+ traders. FICA-verified. No payment processing — you keep control."
   Primary button: "Get started" or "Start trading today".

**UX principles:**
- Desktop-first. Clear hierarchy, calm spacing.
- One primary CTA per section where relevant.
- Trust signals throughout (FICA, verified, contracts, numbers).
- No fake urgency overload; one clear path per persona (Farmer / Buyer / Transporter).
- Feel: world-class SaaS for a serious industry — reliable, fast, and human.

Generate the full landing page layout and key UI components (hero card, pillar cards, transparency grid, how-it-works steps, final CTA) following this structure and design system. Aim for a "billion-dollar product" level of polish: clear, confident, and conversion-focused.
```

## Design System (for Stitch Design Systems panel)

When you click **+ Create** in the Design Systems panel, use the text below. Paste it into Stitch’s design system creator so colors, fonts, and styles stay consistent across generations.

**Copy everything between the quotes:**

```
Farm Feed design system — premium SaaS for a South African grain and feed marketplace. Trust-first, calm, strong hierarchy.

Colors (strict 80/20):
- Primary (80% use): Forest Green — #3D693D. Use for: headers, primary buttons, links, icons, trust badges, card borders, section backgrounds. Hover/dark: #2A4A2A. Light tint: #3D693D at 5–10% opacity for subtle backgrounds.
- Accent (20% use): Harvest Red — #DB4A39. Use sparingly for: urgent CTAs, key highlights, alerts, "live" or limited-offer cues. Hover: #B83A2A.
- Neutrals only otherwise: White #FFFFFF, Gray 50 #F9FAFB, Gray 100 #F3F4F6, Gray 200 #E5E7EB, Gray 600 #4B5563, Gray 900 #111827 (body text), Charcoal #1F2937 (headings). No other brand colors.

Typography:
- Strong hierarchy: one clear H1 per screen, clear H2/H3 for sections. Headings: bold or extra-bold, Forest Green or gray-900. Body: readable size (16–18px), gray-600 or gray-900. No more than 2–3 font weights in one view.
- Feel: professional, confident, agricultural but modern. Premium SaaS — not playful or casual.
- Suggested: clean sans-serif for UI (e.g. Inter, DM Sans, or system -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI"). Optional: one serif only for hero headline if it feels premium.

Spacing and layout:
- Calm spacing: generous padding (e.g. 24–32px for cards, 48–80px between sections). Desktop-first.
- Cards: rounded corners (e.g. 16–24px), light border or soft shadow, white or gray-50 background. Use Forest Green for borders or icon backgrounds on hover/focus.
- Buttons: primary = Forest Green background, white text, min height ~56px, rounded-2xl, bold. Secondary = white background, Forest Green border and text. Accent button = Harvest Red only when urgency is needed.

Components:
- Primary CTA: Forest Green (#3D693D), white text, bold, rounded-2xl, shadow.
- Secondary / outline: white bg, 2px Forest Green border, Forest Green text.
- Cards: white or gray-50, rounded-3xl, subtle border (gray-200 or primary at 10–20% opacity). Icon circles: Forest Green at 10% bg, Forest Green icon.
- Trust elements: small badges, "verified" labels, stats — use Forest Green or gray-900, keep minimal.

Rules: Fewer elements, stronger impact. No generic stock look. Every color choice should reinforce trust and clarity. 80% Forest Green, 20% Harvest Red, rest neutrals.
```
